About TavaGroup

TavaGroup is a family-run business dedicated to producing high-quality garbage bags. Our Strong Bags line offers durability, flexibility, and eco-friendly solutions for households and companies alike. We take pride in our long-standing commitment to sustainability and customer satisfaction.

From small 35L bags to massive 220L industrial options, TavaGroup provides a product for every purpose — ensuring a cleaner, safer tomorrow.
